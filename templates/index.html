Seeing the Future from Above

Mapping Change to Shape a Better Tomorrow

What if we could not only observe how cities transform but also predict their future? This is the vision behind our project, where we explore the rapid urban evolution in Egypt. By combining satellite imagery with advanced artificial intelligence, we aim to decode urban changes and set the stage for future predictions.

About the Project

Our work focuses on analyzing the transformation of urban areas in Egypt over the past eight years, from 2016 to 2024. Through this project, we aim to detect changes in urban landscapes, creating a foundation for future expansion to other regions.

Key Goals

Unveiling Urban Transformations

Using satellite imagery, we reveal the extent and nature of changes occurring across Egypt's urban areas.

AI-Powered Analysis

Leveraging an Encoder-Decoder architecture, our model captures intricate details of urban changes with precision. Additionally, we integrate sequential networks to predict how these zones might evolve in the future, offering a glimpse into tomorrow's urban landscape.

Actionable Insights

Our findings will empower urban planners, architects, and policymakers to align future development with sustainability goals.

Why This Matters

Focus on the Future

Urban transformation in Egypt is a significant driver of the country's vision for the future. By analyzing this development, we create a framework for sustainable growth in emerging cities.

Scalable Solutions

Starting with selected urban zones, our methodology is designed for scalability, enabling its application to other urban and environmental contexts worldwide.

Innovative Urban Planning

The integration of change detection and predictive modeling offers a unique perspective, bridging current analysis with long-term foresight for smarter urban development.

The Vision

We are building a robust pipeline that not only detects past and present changes but also predicts the future. With advanced forecasting techniques, we aim to help cities grow intelligently and sustainably.

This project is more than just technology—it's about vision. By turning satellite imagery into actionable insights, we're laying the groundwork for smarter, more resilient urban growth.

The future of cities is written in the changes of today, and with AI, we're ready to read—and shape it.
